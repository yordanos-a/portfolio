About Me

Hi! My name is Yordanos Ayelework. I am currently a senior doing my Bachelor's of Science at McMurry University. I major in Computer Science and minor in Mathematics and Religion. I work as a Math and Computer Science tutor, as well as the Supplemental Instructor for Programming Fundamentals. Additionally, I am the president of the WarHawk Coders Club.

I am passionate about software engineering and web development, with a strong interest in front-end and back-end development. I thrive on collaborating with others to create projects that solve real world problems.

Outside of Computer Science, my hobbies include books, art, music, and engaging in social gatherings. I love getting to know people and creating new meaningful connections.
