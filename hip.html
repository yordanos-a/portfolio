Health Informatics Platform (HIP)

The Health Informatics Platform (HIP) is a tool developed to analyze and understand the effects of various insulin types and dosages on managing diabetes. Extracting real-world Multivariate, Time-Series Diabetes patients' dataset from the publicly available UC Irvine Machine Learning Repository, the project demonstrates the application of our data modeling and algorithmic techniques in the healthcare domain.

The platform was implemented in Java, using an object-oriented approach. It highlights the intersection of Computer Science and Healthcare, demonstrating the potential to impact real-world medical challenges positively. This work was presented at McMurry University's Symposium and Abilene Christian University's Undergraduate Research, Creativity and Innovation Festival.
